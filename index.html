Social Media Good?

Address it

さあ始めて. Is social media good for companies? Yes, it allows for direct interaction. Companies can get immediate feedback and learn what customers are saying about them.

To use social media, you must:

  1. Decide on platform e.g Twitter
  2. Create an account
  3. Post content
  4. Follow other people
  5. Rinse and repeat
